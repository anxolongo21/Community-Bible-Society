Home

We offer an innovative approach to helping people emerge from the cycle of poverty by providing nutritious meals,
life skills training and after school activities to poorly resourced communities in the Western Cape.

Community Nutrition Programme

Provide access to nutritious food and basic skills to the eldery and unemployed citizens.

Skills and Career Development

Provide fundamental life skills, personal mastery, leadership and workplace skills.
The 6 month programme also offers basic vocational training for unemployed youth.

After-School Programme

Helps boost academic performance, reduce risky behaviors, promote physical health, and provide a safe, structured environment for childrena and youth.

About

The Community Bible Society (CBS) is one of the oldest philanthropic organizations in Khayelitsha.
It is an experienced and trusted organisation partnering with well established corporations and organisations to realise out collective vision of creating a stable and caring society.

Mission

Our Mission is to advance the dignity, equity and prosperity of our people by addressing the fundamental human needs of food, employment and education.

Community

Physical and emotional health, including optimal nutritional health, chronic disease prevention, and food safety is emphasized and supported.

Economic Empowerment

We teach unemployed youth basic life and technical skills necessary for career placement or entrepreneurship.

Creativity

We teach unemployed youth basic life and technical skills necessary for career placement or entrepreneurship.

Visionary Leadership

We connect young leaders with a passion for change with the skills and resources they need to become effective leaders in their communities.
